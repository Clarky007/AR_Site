Kauai Plumbing

Kauai Plumbing provides quality residential and commercial plumbing services to all islands of Hawai’i. The company was founded on generations of experience and built with integrity. Our services include new construction, service and repair, remodel, leak detection of all types of plumbing and gas systems. Whether it’s a leaky faucet, clogged drain, water heater replacement, or new residence our trained plumbers have the knowledge and experience to perform the work and ensure customer satisfaction.
